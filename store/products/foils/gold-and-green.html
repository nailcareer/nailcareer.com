Use this eye catching foil on your nails as inlays or on top of polish. Unleash its full vibrancy with a top coat!

  • For Inlay: Place pieces of foil in between layers of hard gel, acrylic, or hybrids. Place on top of uncured acrylic or before you cure gels. Apply your product of choice on top. Watch it brighten and come to life as you apply a top coat!
